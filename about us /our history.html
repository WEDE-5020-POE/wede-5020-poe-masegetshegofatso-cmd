Our History

Good and positive deeds was established with a deep commitment to serving the elderly members of Greenside Town. The idea began when adriven and smart individual lost her grandmother due to unlimited resoruces to care for her, from that moment she knew she will open an old age home one day.

In its early days, the organization started as a samll initiative driven by volunteers who provided basic assistance such as meals, companionship, and home visits. As the need continued to grow, the vision expanded into creating a fully functional old age home that could offer comprehensive care and long-term support.

Through community support, donations, and partnerships, Good and positive deeds has grown into a trusted organization dedicated to improving the lives of the elderrly. Today, it continues to focus on providing quality care, promoting dignity, and bulding a strong sense of community among its residents.

The organization remains committed to its founding values of compassion, respect, and service, striving to ensure that every elderly individual in Greenside Town has a place they can call home.
